Thursday: Fried Potato Spirals

Description

Fried-potato spirals are like normal fries - just more stylish and even more crunchy!

They are made with an apple corer. Itâ€™s great fun, I promise! :)

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat deep fryer to 350*F. Clean each potato thoroughly, then either use an apple corer or spiral cut the potatoes by hand.
  2. Skewer each potato then fan out the layers down the stick. Place into the deep fryer until covered with oil, and rotate occasionally for even cooking.
  3. Deep fry 3-5 minutes or until the potatoes reach desired crispiness. Drop onto plate lined with a paper towel to dry excess oil. Sprinkle on toppings immediately, serve, and enjoy!
