Saturday: Potato Salad

Description

Potato Salad is a traditional Austrian dish.

Oftentimes, it is served as a side dish. We show you how it is done.

Ingredients

  • 800g potatoes
  • 200ml beef broth
  • 1 onion
  • 5 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 5 tablespoons plant-based oil
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • black pepper and salt
  • wild chive

Steps

  1. Wash and cook the potatoes in slightly salted water, with their peel still on.
  2. When cooked, peel and cut them in thin slices (~5mm). Tip: The potatoes should be as warm as possible.
  3. Mix the cider vinegar, salt, black paper and mustard. Also add the plant-oil slowly.
  4. Dice the onions. :D Tip: If the diced onions smell too strong, you can wash them first in hot water and then with cold water. This way they get milder.
  5. Mix the marinade, potatoes, onions and wild chive carefully with two spoons.
  6. The salad tastes the best after one or two hours - but do not wait too long! Someone else might eat it! :D
